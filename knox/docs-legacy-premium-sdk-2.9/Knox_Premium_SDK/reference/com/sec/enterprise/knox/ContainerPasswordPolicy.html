Administrator can get password size value and take some action based on that. According to protocol, complex chars can be digit or symbol. But WinMobile and other phones have deviated to make password more stricter. Here we do similar. If MinPasswordComplexChars = 1, then at least one digit must be there.If MinPasswordComplexChars > 1, then at least one digit and at least one symbol are required and count of complex characters >= MinPasswordComplexChars
